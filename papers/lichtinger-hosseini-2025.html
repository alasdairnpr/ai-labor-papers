Generative AI as Seniority-Biased Technological Change: Evidence from U.S. Résumé and Job Posting Data

Guy Lichtinger, Seyed Hosseini • 2025 • SSRN Working Paper
employment seniority hiring junior workers descriptive

Summary

Using LinkedIn and Revelio Labs data to track firm-level employment by seniority from 2015-2025. In firms that hire AI integrators, employment for junior workers in AI-exposed roles declines sharply relative to non-adopters. Senior employment doesn't change. Junior decline driven by reduced hiring, not greater firing.

Abstract

Following GenAI adoption, junior employment declines sharply in adopting firms relative to non-adopters, while senior employment remains largely unchanged. The junior decline is concentrated in occupations most exposed to GenAI and is driven by slower hiring rather than increased separations.
