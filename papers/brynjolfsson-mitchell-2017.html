What Can Machine Learning Do? Workforce Implications

Erik Brynjolfsson, Tom Mitchell • 2017 • Science
machine learning tasks skills framework theoretical

Summary

Framework for understanding which tasks are susceptible to ML automation. Identifies prediction as the key capability, and argues displacement effects depend on whether ML complements or substitutes for human judgment.

Abstract

Digital computers have transformed work in almost every sector of the economy, but their full effects on jobs is only beginning to be felt. We discuss what machine learning can and cannot do, and the resistance certain tasks have to automation.
