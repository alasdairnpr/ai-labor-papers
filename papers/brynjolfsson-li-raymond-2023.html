Generative AI at Work

Erik Brynjolfsson, Danielle Li, Lindsey R. Raymond • 2023 • Quarterly Journal of Economics
productivity customer support novice workers chatbots experimental

Summary

Studies staggered deployment of AI chat assistant for ~5,000 customer support agents. Tool increased successfully resolved user chats by 15% per agent per hour. Most of the effect comes from newer & less skilled workers, who see 30% increase in chats resolved/hour.

Abstract

Studied a staggered rollout of an AI assistant for 5,000+ customer support agents. Access to the tool increased issues resolved per hour by ~14% on average (and +34% for novice workers, with minimal gains for experienced workers). The AI helpdesk disseminated best practices, boosted productivity especially for junior staff, improved customer sentiment, and reduced employee turnover.
