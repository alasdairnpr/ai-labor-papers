Assessing early labor market effects of generative AI: Evidence from population data

Antti Kauhanen, Petri Rouvinen • 2024 • Applied Economics Letters
employment wages Finland population data descriptive

Summary

  • Used data on all wage earners in Finland to assess the impact of ChatGPT on the labor market from Nov 2022 - Nov 2024.
  • Find no significant differences in changes in wage or employment levels between more and less exposed occupations.
  • They suggest LLMs may be both complementing and substituting for human labor in the short term, raising productivity in some tasks and replacing humans in others, with close to zero net effect so far.

Abstract

Synthetic DiD by occupation across full Finnish population. Post-ChatGPT no negative short-run employment effects, wages rose relatively more in highly exposed occupations.
