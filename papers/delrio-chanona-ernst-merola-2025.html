AI and Jobs: A review of theory, estimates, and evidence

R. Maria del Rio-Chanona, Ekkehard Ernst, Rossana Merola, Daniel Samaan, Ole Teutloff • 2025 • arXiv preprint
meta analysis productivity employment literature review meta-analysis

Summary

Surveys studies of AI impact on productivity. Finds gains are 20-60% in RCTs, 15-30% in field experiments. In simple tasks, novice workers benefit most. Most recent studies show more substantial declines in demand for novice workers in AI-complementary jobs.

Abstract

Meta-analysis of RCTs, field experiments, and other data. New classification of task complexity. Bigger productivity gains in RCTs than field experiments. Mild evidence of declining demand for junior workers in writing/translation.
