Navigating the Jagged Technological Frontier: Field Experimental Evidence of the Effects of AI on Knowledge Worker Productivity and Quality

Fabrizio Dell'Acqua, Edward McFowland III, Ethan R. Mollick • 2023 • SSRN Working Paper
productivity consulting GPT 4 knowledge work experimental

Summary

RCT with ~750 BCG consultants. For tasks within AI capabilities, consultants with AI access completed 12% more tasks, 25% faster, and 40% better on quality. Effect strongest among below-average consultants. For tasks outside AI frontier, AI users were 19pp less accurate.

Abstract

Field RCT with 750+ consultants given access to GPT-4 vs control. More tasks completed, faster, higher quality, but varied by task and most pronounced for AI amenable tasks.
