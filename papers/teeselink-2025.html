Generative AI and Labor Market Outcomes: Evidence from the United Kingdom

Bouke Klein Teeselink • 2025 • SSRN Working Paper
employment UK job postings entry level quasi-experimental

Summary

Finds the most exposed UK firms experienced a 4.5% fall in total employment relative to firms without exposure, skewed toward junior positions. Most exposed firms posted 16pp fewer job ads, with biggest declines in technical and creative roles.

Abstract

Used U.K. firm and vacancy data (2021–2025) to study differential impacts in firms/jobs with varying exposure to LLM capabilities. Results show high-LLM-exposed firms cut total employment, especially entry-level hiring. These firms sharply pulled back on new hires in technical roles.
