Large Language Models, Small Labor Market Effects

Anders Humlum, Emilie Vestergaard • 2025 • NBER Working Paper No. 33777
employment wages Denmark chatbots quasi-experimental

Summary

Examines labor impact of AI chatbots in Denmark by surveying 25,000 workers and linking responses to government labor records. Finds zero effects of AI chatbot adoption on earnings or hours, even for workers who use AI tools daily.

Abstract

Examined early labor-market impacts of AI chatbot adoption in Denmark by linking firm AI adoption surveys to worker administrative records. Using diff-in-diff, they find essentially no short-run effect on earnings or hours worked – ruling out changes beyond about 2% even two years after adoption.
