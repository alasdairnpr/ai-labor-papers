The Short-Term Effects of Generative Artificial Intelligence on Employment: Evidence from an Online Labor Market

Xiang Hui, Oren Reshef, Luofeng Zhou • 2023 • SSRN Working Paper
employment freelancers Upwork gig economy quasi-experimental

Summary

Uses Upwork data to analyze freelancers before and after release of ChatGPT, DALL-E 2, and Midjourney. ChatGPT reduced monthly job offerings in writing-related occupations by 2% and earnings by 5.2%. Image AI reduced postings by 3.7% and earnings by 9.4%.

Abstract

Upwork panel of freelancers before/after ChatGPT, DALL-E 2, Midjourney releases. In affected categories, freelancers' employment and earnings fell in the short run.
