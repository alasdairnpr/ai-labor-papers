Displacement or Augmentation? The Effects of AI Innovation on Workforce Dynamics and Firm Value

Mark A. Chen, Joanna Wang • 2025 • SSRN Working Paper
patents augmentation displacement innovation quasi-experimental

Summary

  • Uses data on AI related patents in the US from 2007 to 2023.
  • AI innovations related to high-level cognition (i.e., language, decision-making, engagement, learning, or creativity) augmented human labor, while those related to low-level cognition (i.e., perception or motor control) displace it.

Abstract

2007-2023 US patent data: AI innovations related to high-level cognition (language, decision-making, engagement, learning, creativity) augment human labor, while those related to low-level cognition (perception, motor control) displace it.
